Good To Know

Questions, answered

How much will my cleaning cost? +
Standard cleaning is $30/hour and deep cleaning is $50/hour, plus any optional add-ons you choose. Because every space is different, the easiest way to get an exact number is a quick free quote — just call, text, or send a few details about your home and what you'd like done.
What's the difference between standard and deep cleaning? +
Standard cleaning maintains an already well-kept space — dusting, surfaces, kitchens, bathrooms and floors. Deep cleaning is a thorough top-to-bottom reset that tackles baseboards, grout, build-up and the hard-to-reach areas. First-time cleans and move-outs are usually best as a deep clean.
Are you insured? +
Yes — Luna Luxe is fully insured, so you can have complete peace of mind while we care for your space.
Do you offer recurring cleanings? +
Absolutely. Weekly, biweekly and monthly schedules are available and are a favorite for keeping your home effortlessly luxurious. Recurring clients get priority scheduling.
Do you handle Airbnb & rental turnovers? +
Yes! Short-term rental and Airbnb turnovers are one of our specialties — fast, reliable resets with hotel-grade presentation between every guest.
What areas do you serve? +
We proudly serve College Station, Bryan and the surrounding Brazos Valley. Luna also travels to surrounding towns for a small additional fee — just ask when you reach out.
